WHY?!

Why did you buy a Saab?!

The question I get asked every time I tell what car I'm driving these days. It certainly sounds like a bad choice.

The manufacturer doesn't build them anymore since they went out of business in 2011. Parts are getting more and more difficult to come by. They're weird and quirky, build by people who didn't make compromises (hence the bankruptcy).

So why don't just buy a Toyota?

You don't get a Saab because of rational choices, it's all about feeling. It's like having a relationship, with a car. You fall in love and become blind to all the flaws and weird traits. You continue fixing and forgiving to keep it going as long as possible, because it makes you happy.
