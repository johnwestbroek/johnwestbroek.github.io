A few things about John’80

I consider myself fortunate to spend most of my time working on things I’m genuinely interested in.

I tend to approach things as systems. How they work, how they hold together, and how they can be improved over time. That way of thinking runs through my work. I enjoy working with subject matter experts and translating complex ideas into something clear, usable and accessible.

Outside of work, that same curiosity carries over into personal projects and interests. Building and maintaining small systems, writing, drawing, and exploring ideas through this site.
